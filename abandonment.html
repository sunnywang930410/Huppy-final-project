Why are there stray dogs?

Regardless of marital factors, emotional factors, family factors, religious factors... when you have the idea of ​​"abandoning" your pet, it is doomed to wander miserably.

What happens to abandoned dogs...

Hungry

Dogs do not have the hunting ability like cats and can only find food in trash cans.If you accidentally eat toxic substances, choke on garbage, or cannot find food, the dog may die.

Disease

When dogs come into contact with other stray dogs in the wild, they are very likely to be infected with various diseases.

Accident

A recently abandoned dog is likely to be bullied by other stray dogs, hit by a car, accidentally enter a construction site and fall to death, or fall into a rescue trap.

Bad Weather

If a dog is in a weak physical condition and encounters natural disasters such as typhoons, heavy rains, cold snaps, etc., it is very likely to die.

Malicious attacks

There are many evil humans who attack stray dogs with sticks and firecrackers, causing injuries to the dogs.

If you have any hesitation at all, please don’t raise it.

This is a life, not something you can throw away if you don’t want it.
